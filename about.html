CoffeeMan History

We are proud of who we are!

2005

In 2005, as a young woman sitting in a coffee shop, I had an idea of how cool that would be to have my own coffee shop. But at that time, having no resources and no knowledge about how to run a business, the idea was put aside in a far drawer.

2007

One day, fall 2007, I was walking along one of the streets in Boston and was trying to find a coffee shop to warm up and enjoy a cup of coffee. But for about 30 minutes, there were no coffee shops, so I dived in the subway and went home instead. On my way back, I was wondering if I were the only one trying to find a coffee shop in that area.

2008

After that day, I've trying to find a place in that area that would be fit for a coffee shop and on January 10th, I found that place. So the construction begun.

2008

Six months later, on July 10th, we opened the first CoffeeMan shop in Boston.

2011

It took three years, but strating from 2011, we strated opening CoffeeMan shops in other cities and states in the US, cluding Los Angeles, San Francisco, New York and Chicago.

2021

Now having more than 25 shops, we are still continue to develop and grow. We constantly revise our menu and make sure that our customers are satisfied.
